How do I post?

Once you've written up your question or discussion, here are 3 easy steps to make sure your post goes through.

  • Add a topic to make your content easier to find for other members! Start typing in a topic or click the magnifying tool on the right for a list of suggestions.

    • Here are some topic ideas to get you started: News, Recommendations, Community Questions, Photos, Education, Outdoor Activities

  • On NABUR, you can choose to share your post with the greater Cochise County community, or just your neighborhood.

    • You can only post to Communities you already belong to. To see a list of which communities you have joined, and to join more, click here.
  • All that's left to do is click post, and share your content with your community!
